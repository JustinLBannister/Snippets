Disruption does not take a linear path

It ripples cutting across industries, borders and systems. It drives breakthroughs, deepens divides and fractures, old assumptions of stability.

Future‑Defining Themes

Synthetic Everything

Synthetic alternatives will reshape how we build, consume, and create value.

From biology to materials to data, synthetic alternatives are transforming how products are designed, consumed, and valued.

Synthetic innovation is moving from concept to scale—reimagining how we manufacture, heal, and power the world. While unlocking new efficiency and control, these technologies challenge ideas of authenticity, trust, and ownership.

RBC Imagine explores where synthetic technologies will create new markets and redefine value.

Stratified Societies

Economic and social divides are reshaping how societies function and organize.

Growing disparities in wealth, access, and opportunity are creating increasingly stratified societies worldwide.

This theme explores the implications of widening gaps and the emergence of parallel systems serving different societal segments.

Rise of the 8th Continent

The ocean economy emerges as a new frontier for resources and innovation.

As land-based resources face increasing pressure, the ocean presents unprecedented opportunities for energy, food, and materials.

This blue economy revolution is driving technological innovation while raising critical questions about sustainability and governance.

Shifting Superpowers

Global power dynamics are being reshaped by emerging economies and technologies.

Traditional power structures are evolving as new players emerge on the global stage, driven by technological advancement and economic growth.

This shift is redefining international relations, trade patterns, and the very nature of influence in the 21st century.

Crisis Capitalism

Crises are becoming catalysts for rapid economic transformation and opportunity.

From pandemics to climate events, crises are accelerating change and creating new market dynamics at unprecedented speed.

Organizations that can adapt quickly and identify opportunities within disruption are emerging as the new market leaders.
